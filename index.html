Make your life worth remembering forever


Five Thousand Years Ago.... The ancient Sumerian peoples - in what is now modern Kuwait - gifted humanity with one of the greatest inventions of all time: writing. For the first time on the history of our species, we had our eyes opened to revolutionary ideas that would come forth: epic tales that would last for thousands of years, written and codified laws, and the daily recordings of trade and taxes that allow people far away and millenia apart to understand how people lived, and felt.


With Sumer, the power is in your hands to record the only life you will ever live. More than simply a date and a summary, Sumer records three types of behaviors: accomplishments, vices, and virtues. Accomplishments are tasks you set on to complete that day, and carry over to the next if left unfinished. Vices are what you want to avoid on a daily basis, like spending more than three hours a day on Netflix. Virtues are activities you want to do every day, such as exercise.
